Drogo

Drogo came to join our family in 2019 and is a grey runt-of-the-litter Havamalt (a cross between a Havanese and a Maltese.) He hit us with a bang, sleeping in the car like a travelling champ and not fighting it when we dressed him up in a little bow tie! He is totally sweet and just wants to be with his people, curled in their laps, or ramming his tongue up their noses.

Khaleesi

Khaleesi joined us in May of 2020 and is also a Havamalt but she is pearly white, a normal size, and has a very different temperament than Drogo. She definitely can be sweet but doesn't go out of her way to do so. She is more often laying about 2 to 3 feet away from you, just out of reach.

Fun Fact!   When my wife and I got married, our friends sent us a Cameo greeting from the actor who played "Holdor!" from Game of Thrones and he callled out our puppies being named after the show's characters – it was pretty cool!
